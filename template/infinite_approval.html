Unlimited approvals - Brief explanation

Unlimited Approval is a feature that enables users to grant platforms and smart contracts the permission to spend tokens/ coins on your behalf without limit. Normally, when swapping on a specific AMM such as Uniswap, users need to approve the smart contract/ platform to transfer those tokens on their behalf. When swapping a specific amount, you can choose to set an allowance of that exact amount.

When giving these platforms/ smart contracts unlimited approval, users will face 2 main risks: malicious projects and bug exploits. This risk gives hackers the right to use the approved tokens for many lucrative purposes. Therefore, in order to mitigate these risks for users, Coin98 only integrates the Unlimited Approval function with, the top-tier AMMs in the market with transparent information, trustworthy teams, and a large number of users.

Actions you can take to improve security

In this case, the optimal way to enhance security is to eliminate the unlimited approval. On the table below, you will find a list of your approvals. To remove an approval, simply click the button on the right. Alternatively, you can utilize the 'Remove Batch' feature to remove approvals for up to ten contracts simultaneously.
